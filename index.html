Elon Musk

A pioneer in electric vehicles and private space exploration

A timeline of Elon Musk:

  • 1971 - Born in Pretoria, South Africa
  • 1983 - Learned computer programming through a manual, developed and sold his first video game for $500.
  • 1989 - Moved to Canada working physical labor jobs
  • 1990 - Formally began his education at Queen's University in Kingston, Ontario
  • 1992 - Transferred to the University of Pennsylvania where he would graduate with a Bachelor of Science degree in economics and a Bachelor of Arts degree in physics.
  • 1995 - He was accepted to Stanford University in California to begin his Ph.D in materials science, but dropped out after only 2 days to pursue his interested in the Internet boom with his own startup.
  • 1999 - He and others founded Zip2, a web software company that would later sell for $307 million of which he owned a 7% share of the company.
  • 1999 - Musk co-founded X.com, later renamed to Paypal, which was later bought by eBay for $1.5 billion of which Elon owned an 11.7% share.
  • 2002 - Founded SpaceX which he remains CEO of.
  • 2004 - Became a majority shareholder in Tesla, Inc. where he assumed leadership as CEO which boosted his financial wealth to become the worlds richest man as of 2021.
